Before you can register for your classes at Davenport University, you must first set up an advisory session.

During your session, you will meet one-on-one with your advisor to plan your course schedule. Your advisor can also introduce you to the university’s support offices—including Financial Aid and Career Services—to help ensure you have all the necessary tools to make your transition to college life a successful one.
